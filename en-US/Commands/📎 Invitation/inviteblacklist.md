# inviteblacklist

> Allows you to blacklist a member of the invitation system.

<br>

## Usage :

*inviteblacklist <option> [@member]

## Examples :

*inviteblacklist add @member,
<br>*inviteblacklist remove @member,
<br>*inviteblacklist list
